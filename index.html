About Us

Rotaract clubs are part of a global effort to bring peace and international understanding to the world.

The purpose of Rotaract is to provide an opportunity for young men and women to enhance the knowledge and skills to address physical and social needs of the communities.

We are undergraduates of Maulana Azad National Institute of Bhopal united for a good cause.
